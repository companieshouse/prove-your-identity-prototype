Identity verification for Companies House outcome

Your case reference number: HW123456

We have reviewed the information you submitted when you applied to verify your identity.

Outcome: Unsuccessful

You have not been able to verify your identity for Companies House. For security reasons, we cannot tell you the reason why.

You will not be able to try to verify your identity using the 'Provide evidence of your identity to Companies House' service again.

Next steps

You'll need to find a different way to try and verify your identity. You have 2 options.

Option 1

Use the 'Verify your identity for Companies House' service, which uses GOV.UK One Login to verify your identity. It is free of charge.

GOV.UK One Login will ask you some simple questions to find the best way for you to verify your identity. You must provide answers about yourself, not your company. Depending on your answers, you'll then be guided to verify in one of the following ways:

  • with an app
  • by answering security questions online
  • by entering your details from your photo ID on GOV.UK One Login first, then going to a participating Post Office

Go to the 'Verify your identity for Companies House' service:
https://www.gov.uk/guidance/verify-your-identity-for-companies-house

Option 2

Ask an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP) if they can verify your identity. This is also known as an authorised agent.

Find out more about verifying using an ACSP:
https://identity.company-information.service.gov.uk/identity-verification/verifying-with-authorised-corporate-service-provider
