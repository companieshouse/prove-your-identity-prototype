What happens if you successfully verify your identity

You’ll get a unique identifier known as a Companies House personal code. The code is personal to you, not your company or a company you work for. You'll need the code to comply with identity verification requirements.

You can still verify using an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)

If you do not want to wait to verify by providing evidence to Companies House, you can use an ACSP instead. This is also known as a Companies House authorised agent.

It can be quicker to verify your identity using an ACSP.
