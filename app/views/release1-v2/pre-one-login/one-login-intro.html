We’re taking you to verify your identity with GOV.UK

The fastest way to verify is with the GOV.UK mobile app

GOV.UK One Login allows you to download an app that scans your biometric ID.

The mobile app:

  • is fast and simple
  • works with any up-to-date mobile phone
  • is secure - the app only communicates data, it doesn’t store anything

You can delete the app when you've finished.

Verifying without the mobile app

You can also verify your identity with GOV.UK One Login online without the app. You will need to answer a few questions about yourself to confirm who you are.

If you cannot verify online, GOV.UK One Login will tell you how to verify in-person at the Post Office.
