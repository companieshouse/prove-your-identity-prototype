Ways you can prove your identity for Companies House

Online

You can use the Verify your identity for Companies House service to verify online if you have the required identity documents.

You'll need a (one of the following):

  • biometric passport from any country
  • UK photo driving licence (full or provisional)
  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP) (opens in a new tab)
What is a biometric passport?

Biometric passports have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through electronic gates at airports and international train stations.

All UK passports issued after 2006 are biometric.

In person

If you live in the UK, you can use the Verify your identity for Companies House service to find out how to verify in person at the Post Office.

Using a Companies House authorised agent

If you do not have a UK home address or any required identity documents, you can ask a Companies House authorised agent to verify your identity on your behalf. You can do this from any country, but your agent must be registered in the UK.

More about verifying with a Companies House authorised agent.

What happens if you do not verify your identity

You need to successfully prove who you are to Companies House. If you do not verify your identity in time, you are committing an offence.

You could:

  • get a maximum fine of £XXX
  • be stopped from acting as a director
  • be unable to make any filings for your company or entity
  • be unable to start a new company or entity
