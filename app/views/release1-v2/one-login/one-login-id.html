UK photocard driving licence

You can use a UK driving licence if it:

  • has your photo
  • has not expired

It can be a full or provisional UK driving licence.

UK passport

You can use any UK passport

Non-UK passport with a biometric chip

You can use a non-UK passport if it:

  • has a biometric chip
  • has not expired

To check if your passport has a biometric chip, look for the rectangular biometric chip symbol on the front cover

UK biometric resident permit

You can use a UK biometric permit (BRP) that has not expired.
