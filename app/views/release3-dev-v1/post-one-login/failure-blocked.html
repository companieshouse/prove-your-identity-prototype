You cannot use GOV.UK One Login to verify your identity

You have not been able to prove who you are using GOV.UK One Login. You will not be able to verify your identity using the mobile phone app, online, or in person at the Post Office.

Next steps

Check the guidance and select 'Ways to verify your identity' to find other ways you can try and verify.

What happens if you do not verify

There will be a transition period for existing directors and people with significant control (PSCs), so that you have time to do what the regulations require. Find out when you need to verify your identity.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "If you do not comply with identity verification requirements on time, you may be committing an offence and may have to pay a financial penalty or fine.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

You will also be unable to:

  • make any filings for your company or entity
  • start a new company or entity
  • register as a Companies House authorised agent

Directors

If you continue to act as a director after the deadline without verifying, you will be committing an offence. The company (or entity) and all directors may also be committing an offence.

People with significant control (PSCs)

If you continue to be a PSC after the deadline without verifying, you may be committing an offence.

If you still need help

Contact GOV.UK One Login if you still need help. For security reasons, they will not be able to tell you why you cannot verify using GOV.UK One Login.

Do not contact Companies House, as we are not able to help with this.
