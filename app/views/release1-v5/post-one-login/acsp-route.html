Verifying with a Companies House authorised agent

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "There will be a transition period so that you have time to do what the regulations require. We cannot give an exact date for when you'll be able to verify with an authorised agent yet, but it will be before the transition period ends. " }) }}

The agent must be registered with Companies House as an authorised agent - also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

If you use an agent, your identity will only be verified for Companies House, not any other government service or department.

Who can be an authorised agent

Authorised agents could be:

  • accountants
  • solicitors
  • company formation agents
