Verify your identity for Companies House

Anyone who runs or files for a company needs to verify their identity for Companies House. You only need to verify once, even if you have multiple roles for different companies.

You can verify yourself online with GOV.UK if you have one of the following types of photo ID:

  • biometric passport from any country
  • UK photo driving licence (full or provisional)
  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP)

If you do not have any of these types of identity but you live in the UK, GOV.UK will tell you how to verify in person at the Post Office.

What is a biometric passport?

Biometric passports have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through electronic gates at airports and international train stations.

All UK passports issued after 2006 are biometric.

I do not have any of the required identity documents

You'll soon be able to ask a Companies House authorised agent (such as an accountant or solicitor) to verify your identity on your behalf.

You’ll need to provide suitable identity documents. Find out which documents you can use to verify your identity with an authorised agent.

! Warning Fraud warning: do not let others use your identity

Do not sell or give away your identity to set up or run a company. Criminals can take out loans and other debt in your name, and you will be legally responsible.
