What is a Companies House personal code?

Your Companies House personal code connects your verified identity to our records. This is so that we know the correct person is linked to the name we have on file. The code is personal to you, not your company or a company you work for.

You'll need your personal code:

  • as part of some other company filings, such as the confirmation statement
  • if you are appointed as a director
  • if you are appointed as a person with significant control (PSC)
