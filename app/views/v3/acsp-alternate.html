Ask a UK authorised agent to verify on your behalf

You currently do not meet the requirements to use GOV.UK One Login.

Your agent must be:

  • based in the UK
  • registered with Companies House as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)

If your agent is not yet authorised

Agents can register with Companies House in order to become authorised.

What happens next

When your authorised agent has agreed to verify your identity on your behalf, they will ask you to provide proof of your address and identity.

Who can be an authorised agent

An authorised agent must be regulated under the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations. They could be:

  • accountants
  • solicitors
  • company formation agents
  • notaries
