Verify your identity for Companies House

You need to verify your identity if you're a company director (or equivalent), a person with significant control (PSC) or someone who files with Companies House.

How to verify your identity

You can choose to verify in one of these ways:

  • Use the GOV.UK One Login service to verify yourself
  • Ask an authorised agent (usually an accountant or solicitor) to verify on your behalf

What you’ll need:

  • Photo ID (a passport or driving licence)
  • Your current home address
