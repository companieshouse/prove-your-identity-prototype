Choose a way to verify

Option 1: verify with GOV.UK online

You can use GOV.UK One Login if you have any of the following:

  • a home address in the UK
  • a biometric passport from any country
  • a UK photo driving licence

If you do not have a biometric ID but you live in the UK, GOV.UK will guide you through an in-person service.

When your identity is verified with One Login, it will also be valid for use with HMRC and the DWP.

{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "What is a biometric passport?", text: "Biometric passports have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through electronic gates at airports and international train stations." }) }}

Option 2: verify with an authorised agent

Everyone can ask an authorised agent (such as an accountant or solicitor) to verify their identity on their behalf. You can do this from any country, but your agent must be registered in the UK.

If you verify with your authorised agent, your identity will only be verified for Companies House.
