What happens next

You’ve told us your confirmation statement due date is 15 May 2026. If this date has not passed yet, we’ll aim to email you by this date at example@email.com with information about how to verify your identity. You should check your inbox regularly, including your spam folder. We cannot guarantee we'll be able to contact you by the date of your next confirmation statement.

If this date has already passed, we’ll email you as soon as we are able to.

! Warning It could be up to 18 weeks before we email you. You should print or save this page so that you can refer back to this information.

We'll let you know which identity documents you'll need when we email you. You'll be able to use a passport if you have one. If you do not already have a passport, and you are able to get one, you may wish to start the passport application process now.

When you have successfully verified, you’ll get a unique identifier known as a Companies House personal code. The code is personal to you, not your company or a company you work for. You'll need the code to comply with identity verification requirements.

You can still verify using an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)

If you do not want to wait to verify by providing evidence to Companies House, you can use an ACSP instead. These are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

It can be quicker to verify your identity using an ACSP.

If you're a person with significant control (PSC)

If you're not going to be able to verify in time, use the Provide identity verification details for a PSC service to request an extension (opens in new tab). This will give you more time to provide your personal code and verification statement to us.
