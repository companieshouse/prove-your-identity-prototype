Who can vouch for your identity

Someone can vouch for you if they meet the following conditions.

They must:

  • be in a position of authority in their community - for example, a teacher, doctor, solicitor or company director
  • have known you for X years
  • have verified their own identity using GOV.UK One Login
  • be based in the UK at the time of vouching
  • be able to vouch that you look like who you claim to be

They must not:

  • be related to you by birth or marriage, or another family relationship (such as adoption)
  • be in a relationship with you
  • live with you

Check the complete list of people who can vouch for someone's identity. It is your responsibility to find someone who will vouch for you.
