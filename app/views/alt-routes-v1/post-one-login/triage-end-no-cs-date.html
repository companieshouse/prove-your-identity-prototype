You've told us you want to provide evidence of your identity to Companies House

What happens next

We'll aim to email you at example@email.com as soon as possible with information about how to verify your identity. You should check your inbox regularly, including your spam folder.

If your confirmation statement is due soon, we cannot guarantee we'll be able to contact you by this date.

! Warning It could be up to 18 weeks before we email you.

We'll let you know which identity documents you'll need when we email you. However, you will definitely need one of the following:

  • passport
  • driving licence
  • national identity card

If you don't have any of these documents, you may wish to start the application process now.

You can still verify using an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)

If you do not want to wait to verify by providing evidence to Companies House, you can use an ACSP instead. These are also known as Companies House authorised agents.

It can be quicker to verify your identity using an ACSP.
