How to find the next confirmation statement date

You can find it by searching for the company on the Companies House register (opens in a new tab).

If you are an officer of more than one company the enter the confirmation statement date that comes first.

For example:

  • COMPANY A LTD's confirmation statement is due on 23 April 2026
  • COMPANY B LTD's confirmation statement is due on 16 July 2026

As COMPANY A LTD's confirmation statement is due first, you would need to enter 23 4 2026.
