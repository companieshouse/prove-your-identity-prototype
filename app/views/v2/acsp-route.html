Verify with authorised agent

Ask an authorised agent (such as an accountant or solicitor) to verify your identity with Companies House on your behalf.

Your agent must be registered with Companies House as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)).

If your agent is not yet authorised

Agents can register with Companies House in order to become an authorised agent.

What happens next?

When your authorised agent has agreed to verify your identity on your behalf, they will ask you to provide proof of your address and identity. See which documents you can use in the UK .
