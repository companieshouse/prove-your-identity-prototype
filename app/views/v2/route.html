Who can use the GOV.UK verification service

You can use this online service if you have any of the following:

  • a home address in the UK
  • a biometric passport from any country
  • a UK photo driving licence

If you do not have a biometric ID but you live in the UK, GOV.UK will guide you through an in-person service.

Who can use an authorised agent

Everyone can ask an authorised agent to verify their identity on their behalf. You do not need to live in the UK or have a biometric ID.

If you verify with your authorised agent, your identity will be verified for Companies House only.
