You can use the online service if:

Who can use an authorised agent

Everyone can ask an authorised agent to verify their ID on their behalf. You do not need to be a UK resident or have a biometric ID.

Most accountants who file on behalf of companies are authorised agents.

If you verify with your authorised agent, your identity will be verified for Companies House only.
