Verify your identity with Companies House

You need to verify your identity if you are a company director (and equivalent) or person with significant control (PSC) registered with Companies House.

How to verify your identity

You can:

  • use the online GOV.UK ID verification service
  • ask an authorised agent (eg. your accountant or solicitor) to verify on your behalf

What you’ll need:

  • Photo ID (a passport or driving licence)
  • Proof of your address
