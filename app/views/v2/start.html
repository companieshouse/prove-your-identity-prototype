Verify your identity with Companies House

You need to verify your identity if you're a company director (or equivalent) or a person with significant control (PSC) registered with Companies House.

How to verify your identity

You can choose to verify in one of these ways:

  • Use the online GOV.UK ID verification service
  • Ask an authorised agent (usually your accountant or solicitor) to verify on your behalf

What you’ll need:

  • Photo ID (a passport or driving licence)
  • Proof of your address
