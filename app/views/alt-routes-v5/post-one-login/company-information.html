Enter the company number.
How to find your company number and confirmation statement date
  1. Search for the company name on the Companies House register (opens in new tab).
  2. Select the company name.
  3. You'll find the confirmation statement date in the 'overview' tab (it's the first date shown in the 'confirmation statement' section). The company number is also on this screen.
! Warning The link to search the register opens in a new tab. If you select the link, do not close this page. If you do, you will have to start again.

If you are a director of more than one company, enter the confirmation statement date that comes first.

For example:

  • ABC LTD's confirmation statement is due on 23 April 2026
  • XYZ LTD's confirmation statement is due on 16 July 2026

As ABC LTD's confirmation statement is due first, you would need to enter 23 4 2026.
