Verify your identity at Companies House

Use this service to verify your identity so that you can set up, run, own or control a company registered at Companies House.

What'll you'll need

You'll need a:

  • Companies House account - we’ll ask you to sign in or create one
  • GOV.UK One Login - we’ll ask you to sign in or create one

UK residents

People who live in the UK will need:

  • a form of photo ID, such as a biometric passport or a driving licence
  • to tell us your home address (you might also need your previous address if you’ve recently moved)

Non-UK residents

People who do not live in the UK will need:

  • a biometric passport
{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "What is a biometric passport?", text: "Biometric passports (or ‘ePassports’) have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through eGates at airports and train stations." }) }}

We’ll only use the information you give us to make sure that you are who you say you are.

If you do not have the information required to use this service, you’ll need to verify your identity in a different way (to be confirmed).

Alternatively, you can verify your identity through an agent, known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
