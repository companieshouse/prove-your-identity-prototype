Verify your identity at Companies House.

Use this service to verify your identity so that you can set up, run, own or control a company registered at Companies House.

What you'll need

You'll need:

  • a Companies House account - we’ll ask you to sign in or create one
  • a GOV.UK One Login - we’ll ask you to sign in or create one
  • to tell us your home address

UK residents

People who live in the UK will need a form of photo ID, such as a biometric passport or a driving licence.

Non-UK residents

People who do not live in the UK will need a biometric passport.

You can also verify your identity through an agent, known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

It takes around 10 minutes to use this service.
