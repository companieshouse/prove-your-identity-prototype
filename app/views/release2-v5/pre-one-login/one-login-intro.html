We’re taking you to verify your identity with GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK will ask you some simple questions to find the best way for you to verify your identity. Depending on the answers you provide, you’ll then be guided to use one of the following routes:

Route 1: Use the GOV.UK mobile phone app

This is the fastest way to verify. GOV.UK One Login allows you to download an app that scans your biometric ID.

The mobile app:

  • is fast and simple
  • works with any up-to-date mobile phone
  • is secure - the app only communicates data, it doesn’t store anything

You can delete the app when you've finished.

Check if your mobile phone can use the GOV.UK app

You’ll need access to a phone with a working camera. It must be an:

  • iPhone 7 or newer running iOS 13 or higher
  • Android phone (for example, Samsung or Google Pixel) running 10 or higher

You can usually find out which version your phone is running within 'Settings'.

Route 2: Verify online without the mobile phone app

If you cannot verify using the mobile phone app, you can verify online without it. You'll need to answer a few questions about yourself to confirm who you are.

Route 3: Verify in person

If you cannot verify using the mobile phone app or online, GOV.UK One Login will tell you how to verify in person at the Post Office.
