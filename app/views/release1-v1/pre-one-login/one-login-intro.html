We’re taking you to verify your identity with GOV.UK

With the GOV.UK mobile app

If you verify online, you can download an app to scan your biometric ID. It’s the fastest and easiest way to verify. It’s secure to use any up-to-date smart phone, the app doesn’t store your information.

Without the GOV.UK mobile app

You can continue without using the app. You will be asked some questions about your financial history to verify your identity.

If you cannot verify online

GOV.UK One Login will guide you through an in-person alternative route.

When you have verified your identity successfully, we'll take you back to Companies House to continue.

GOV.UK One Login and your personal data

The GOV.UK ID Check app only reads your biometric data and communicates this securely to GOV.UK One Login. The data is not stored on the mobile device and your data is secure.

Read more about how GOV.UK One Login handle your data.
