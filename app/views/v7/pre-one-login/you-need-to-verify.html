You need to verify your identity for Companies House

How to verify your identity

You can verify yourself online with GOV.UK if you have any one of the following:

  • a home address in the UK or;
  • a biometric passport from any country or;
  • a UK photo driving licence or;
  • a UK biometric resident permit (BRP)

If you do not have a biometric ID but you live in the UK, GOV.UK will guide you through an in-person service.
