Make sure you have the required identity documents and try again.

To verify with GOV.UK One Login online, you need (any of the following):

  • a biometric passport from any country
  • a UK photo driving licence (full or provisional)
  • a UK biometric residence permit (BRP)
What is a biometric passport?
Biometric passports have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through electronic gates at airports and international train stations.

All UK passports issued after 2006 are biometric.
