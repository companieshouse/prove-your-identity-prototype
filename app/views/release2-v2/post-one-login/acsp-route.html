Verifying with a Companies House authorised agent

An authorised agent can verify your identity for Companies House on your behalf.

The agent must be registered with Companies House as an authorised agent - also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

If you use an agent, your identity will only be verified for Companies House, not any other government service or department.

Authorised agents will charge a fee for their services.

If your agent is not yet authorised

Agents must register with Companies House in order to become authorised.

Who can be an authorised agent

Authorised agents could be:

  • accountants
  • solicitors
  • company formation agents

What happens next

When your authorised agent has agreed to verify your identity on your behalf, they will ask you to provide proof of your address and identity.
