Ways you can prove your identity for Companies House

If you have photo ID

You can try proving your identity with GOV.UK One Login again.

If you do not have photo ID

You can ask a Companies House authorised agent (such as an accountant or solicitor) to verify your identity on your behalf. You can do this from any country, but your agent must register with Companies House in order to become authorised. They must also be registered in the UK. You’ll need to provide suitable identity documents.

What happens if you do not verify

There will be a transition period so that you have time to do what the regulations require. Find out when you need to verify your identity.

You will also be unable to:

  • make any filings for your company or entity
  • start a new company or entity
  • register as a Companies House authorised agent

PSCs

If you continue to be a PSC after the deadline without verifying, you will be committing an offence.

Directors

If you continue to act as a director after the deadline without verifying, you will be committing an offence. The company (or entity) and all directors may also be committing an offence.
